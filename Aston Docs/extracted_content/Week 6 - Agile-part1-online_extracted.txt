• Also known as a 'stand-up meeting', this is a short but essential 
daily meeting involving everyone working on the sprint.
–
Updates on the previous day are shared
–
Plans for the day ahead are shared
–
Anything that has been learned since the last meeting, including problems, is 
discussed
• Run by an individual titled 'Scrum Master'
• An opportunity to reflect and plan, and ensure work is both 
synchronised and relevant to the sprint goal

98
• The output of every sprint, which should be a deliverable, 
functional, usable product
• Each increment builds on all previous increments; the 
next increment should comprise everything that existed 
already, plus everything from the latest sprint
• Each increment should be a working version of the 
product that could, if necessary, be shipped.
